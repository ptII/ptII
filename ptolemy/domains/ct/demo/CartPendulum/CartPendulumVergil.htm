CartPendulum

This page contains an applet that should appear as a separate window.

This model models a pendulum on a moving cart. The card moves in x dimension and the pendulum moves in x-z plane. Double-click the right yellow block for a graphic explanation. Pendulum collides with the surface of the cart and bounces back with some energy loss, which is specified by the lambda parameter. There is no extra force except the gravity force applied on the pendulum. This model is a Zeno model. We use two states for pre-Zeno and post-Zeno dynamics respectively.
