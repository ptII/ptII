Algorithms for manipulation and analyzing mathematical graphs. These algorithms support scheduling and analysis of Ptolemy II models.

Documentation for the ptolemy.graph package may be found in Volume 2 of the Ptolemy Design Document, available at http://ptolemy.eecs.berkeley.edu/ptolemyII/designdoc.htm.

Significant portions of the ptolemy.graph package were developed by Professor Shuvra S. Bhattacharyya and his group.
