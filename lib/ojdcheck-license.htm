License for Ojdcheck

Ojdcheck is used to point out problems with Javadoc comments. Charles Shelton (Bosch) enhanced Ojdcheck to handle enums, whereas Doccheck does not handle enums.

To check out the tree, use: 

svn co https://source.eecs.berkeley.edu/svn/chess/ojdcheck
Or, if you have a cvs/svn ssh read/write account: 
svn co svn+ssh://source.eecs.berkeley.edu/chess/ojdcheck
